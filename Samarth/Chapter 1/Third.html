The Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE) is an autonomous board of
education in the state of Maharashtra, India. It designs and implements diploma, post diploma and
advanced diploma programs to affiliated institutions. Diploma courses are an excellent choice as
they allow transferable credits towards higher education such as a degree or master's
program. MSBTE loacted at Government Polytechnic Building, 4th floor, 49, Kherwadi, Bandra (E),
Mumbai-400051.
